<p><p>Short-wave length thermal cameras operate in the range of 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers and are commonly used for high-resolution imaging in security applications. Mid-wave length thermal cameras operate in the range of 3 to 5 micrometers and are ideal for detecting human body temperatures. Long-wave length thermal cameras operate in the range of 8 to 14 micrometers and are suitable for long-range detection and tracking. Each type of thermal camera offers unique capabilities and features to effectively meet various security needs.</p></p>
