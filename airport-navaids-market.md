Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) measures the slant range distance between the aircraft and a ground beacon. Non-Directional Beacon (NDB) provides non-directional radio signals for navigation. VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) provides aircraft with radial orientation information. Ground-Based Augmentation System (GBAS) enhances GPS for precise landing guidance. Other systems include radar and microwave landing systems.</p></p>
