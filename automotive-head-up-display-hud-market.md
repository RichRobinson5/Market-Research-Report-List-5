<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Head-Up Display (HUD) technology is available in two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD systems display information directly on the windshield in the driver's line of sight, eliminating the need for the driver to look away from the road. Combiner Projected HUD systems project information onto a separate transparent display in the driver's field of vision. Both types of HUD technology provide important information such as speed, navigation directions, and alerts, enhancing driver safety and convenience.</p></p>
