<p><p>Automotive airbag textiles are primarily classified into two types - flat airbag fabric and OPW (One Piece Woven) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is made from multiple layers of woven material, providing strength and durability. Meanwhile, OPW airbag fabric is a single-layer woven material known for its lighter weight and superior performance in terms of impact resistance. Both types of airbag textiles play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and effectiveness of airbag systems in vehicles.</p></p>
