<p><p>The TV wall mounts market can be divided into four main types: Full Motion, Fixed, Tilt, and Fixed Easy-mount. Full Motion mounts offer the most flexibility, allowing the TV to be swiveled, tilted, and extended away from the wall. Fixed mounts keep the TV close to the wall and are ideal for spaces with limited room. Tilt mounts allow for vertical angle adjustments to reduce glare. Fixed Easy-mounts are designed for easy installation with a simple, fixed position against the wall. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
