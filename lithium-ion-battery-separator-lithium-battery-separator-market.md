<p><p>Lithium-Ion Battery Separator market is segmented into two major types: Bilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator and Trilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator. The Bilayer separator consists of two layers of polymer material that helps in preventing short circuits and enhancing the battery's overall performance. On the other hand, the Trilayer separator consists of three layers, offering better thermal stability and higher mechanical strength. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries in various applications.</p></p>
