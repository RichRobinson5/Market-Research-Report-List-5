<p><strong>The Vibration Welding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Orbital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vibration welding machines are available in two main types - linear and orbital. Linear vibration welding machines utilize linear reciprocating motion to create frictional heat between two parts to be joined. This type is commonly used for joining larger, flat or irregularly shaped parts. On the other hand, orbital vibration welding machines use a circular motion to generate heat for welding. This type is ideal for joining smaller, more complex parts that require a high level of precision and control. Both types offer efficient and reliable welding solutions for various industries.</p></p>
