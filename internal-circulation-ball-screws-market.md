<p><p>Internal Circulation Ball Screws are classified into two main types - Single Nut and Double Nut. Single Nut Ball Screws have only one nut that moves along the screw shaft, providing linear motion. On the other hand, Double Nut Ball Screws have two nuts on the same screw shaft, allowing for higher load capacity and better precision. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications where precise linear motion is required, such as in machine tools, robots, and automation systems.</p></p>
