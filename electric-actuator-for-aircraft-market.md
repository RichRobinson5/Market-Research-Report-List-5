<p><ul><li>Linear Motion</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric actuators for aircraft can be categorized into two main types: linear motion and rotary motion actuators. </p><p>Linear motion actuators are used to convert electrical energy into linear mechanical motion, providing straight-line movement for various aircraft components such as landing gear and cargo doors. </p><p>On the other hand, rotary motion actuators convert electrical energy into rotary mechanical motion, enabling rotational movement for applications like throttle control and flap adjustments. Both types of actuators play essential roles in ensuring smooth and efficient operation of aircraft systems.</p></p>
