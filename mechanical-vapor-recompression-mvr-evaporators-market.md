<p><p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) Evaporators are used in two main types of markets: Compressed Steam System and Water Vapor Distillation System. In the Compressed Steam System, MVR evaporators use high-pressure steam to compress vapor, increasing its temperature and reducing the energy needed for evaporation. On the other hand, Water Vapor Distillation System utilizes the vaporization and condensation of water to separate impurities, making it ideal for water treatment and desalination applications. Both markets offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for evaporation processes.</p></p>
