<p><strong>The Video Camera Gimbal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Level Gimbal</li><li>All-round Gimbal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video camera gimbals are broadly categorized into two types: Level gimbals and All-round gimbals. Level gimbals ensure that the camera remains centered and level at all times, providing stable and smooth footage. On the other hand, All-round gimbals offer more flexibility in terms of movement, allowing the camera to tilt, pan, and rotate in all directions. Both types cater to different shooting styles and preferences, with level gimbals ideal for stationary shots and all-round gimbals suited for dynamic and creative filming angles.</p></p>
