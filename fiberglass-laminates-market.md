<p><p>Fiberglass laminates are primarily classified into two main types based on the type of plastic used in their composition: thermosetting plastic products and thermoplastic plastic products. Thermosetting plastic products are cured and hardened through a heating process, making them durable and resistant to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, thermoplastic plastic products can be melted and reshaped multiple times, enhancing their flexibility and ease of use. Both types of fiberglass laminates offer unique properties and applications in various industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
