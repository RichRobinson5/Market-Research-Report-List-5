<p><p>Casing elevators are essential tools in the oil and gas industry used to lift and lower casing strings during drilling operations. There are two main types of casing elevators: side door and counterpart. Side door elevators have a hinged side door that allows for easy placement and removal of the casing, while counterpart elevators have a split bowl design for more secure gripping of the casing. Both types are widely used in the market and offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of the drilling operation.</p></p>
