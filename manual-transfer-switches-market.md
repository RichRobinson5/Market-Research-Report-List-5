<p><ul><li>Panel Mounted</li><li>Din Rail Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Panel Mounted Manual Transfer Switches are directly installed on the electrical panel and are generally used for larger electrical systems. They provide a compact and easy-to-use solution for transferring power sources. On the other hand, Din Rail Mounted Manual Transfer Switches are mounted on a standard DIN rail and are typically used for smaller electrical systems or in locations where space is limited. They offer a convenient and space-saving option for transferring between power sources. Both types of switches provide a reliable and safe way to switch between power sources in case of an outage.</p></p>
