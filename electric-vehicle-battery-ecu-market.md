Centralized systems involve a single control unit managing all functions of the battery pack. In contrast, Modular systems consist of multiple control units that can be individually managed, allowing for more customizable configurations. Distributed systems utilize separate control units for different sections of the battery pack, offering increased flexibility and efficiency.
