<p><p>Radial tires and bias tires are two main types of tires in the market. Radial tires have their ply cords arranged perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better fuel efficiency, long tread life, and improved handling. On the other hand, bias tires have their ply cords crisscrossed at a diagonal angle, offering a smoother ride and increased load-bearing capacity. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of vehicles and applications. Customers have the option to choose between these two types based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
