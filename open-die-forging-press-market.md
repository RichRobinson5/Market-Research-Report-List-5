<p><p>Open die forging press market can be categorized into different types based on their operation mechanisms. Push-down forging presses utilize hydraulic or mechanical force to push the upper die towards the lower die, while pull-down type forging presses use the reverse action by pulling the upper die down. Other market segments in open die forging press industry may include eccentric gear presses, friction screw presses, and hydraulic presses. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different forging applications.</p></p>
