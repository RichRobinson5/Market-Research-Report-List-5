<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers are available in different market types based on the number of frequency bands they support. Quad-band routers operate on four bands, maximizing coverage and speed. Each market type offers a different level of performance and capability, allowing consumers to choose the best option based on their specific needs and budget.</p></p>
