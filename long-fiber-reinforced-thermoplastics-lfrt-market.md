<p><p>Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastics (LFRT) are composite materials with long fibers embedded in a thermoplastic matrix. In the market, there are two main types of LFRT based on their carbon fiber source - Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based and Pitch-based. PAN-based LFRT offers high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for structural applications. Pitch-based LFRT, on the other hand, provides excellent thermal stability and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. Both types of LFRT cater to different market segments based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
