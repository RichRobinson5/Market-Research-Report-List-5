Commuter Aerobridges are designed for smaller aircraft, providing a more suitable boarding option. Dual Aerobridges are used for larger aircraft, allowing for simultaneous boarding and deboarding. Nose-Loader Aerobridges are specifically designed to accommodate aircraft with front loading doors, providing more efficient boarding processes. Each type caters to different aircraft sizes and requirements in the aviation industry.</p></p>
