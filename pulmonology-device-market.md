<p><p>The pulmonology device market consists of various types of devices including pulmonary biopsy devices, endobronchial ultrasound (EBUS) needles, airway stents, stainless steel stents, single-use bronchoscopes, and others. Pulmonary biopsy devices are used for obtaining tissue samples from the lungs for diagnosis. EBUS needles are used for biopsy guidance in lung nodules and lymph nodes. Airway stents are used to keep the airways open in patients with obstructive respiratory conditions. Stainless steel stents are used for long-term airway support. Single-use bronchoscopes are disposable devices for one-time use in bronchoscopic procedures.</p></p>
