<p><p>Vacant gelatin capsules are available in two main types: Gastric Soluble and Enteric. Gastric Soluble capsules dissolve quickly in the stomach, releasing the contents for immediate absorption. On the other hand, Enteric capsules have a special coating that prevents them from dissolving in the stomach, allowing them to pass through to the intestines before releasing the contents. Depending on the intended purpose and requirements of the medication, either type of vacant gelatin capsule can be chosen for optimal effectiveness.</p></p>
