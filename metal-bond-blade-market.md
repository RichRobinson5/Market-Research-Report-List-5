<p><p>Metal bond blade market is segmented into three types based on the grit used in the blades - SD (Synthetic Diamond) Grit Type, SDC (Synthetic Diamond Coated) Grit Type, and CBN (Cubic Boron Nitride) Grit Type. SD Grit Type blades use synthetic diamond particles for cutting hard materials like granite. SDC Grit Type blades have a layer of synthetic diamond coating on the blade for improved cutting performance. CBN Grit Type blades use cubic boron nitride particles for cutting applications requiring high precision and durability.</p></p>
