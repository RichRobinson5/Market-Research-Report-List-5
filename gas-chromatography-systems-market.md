<p><strong>The Gas Chromatography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid Chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid Chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas Chromatography Systems are commonly classified into two main types: Gas-solid Chromatography (GSC) and Gas-liquid Chromatography (GLC). In GSC, the stationary phase is a solid material, while in GLC, the stationary phase is a liquid coated on a solid support. GSC is mainly used for analyzing inorganic compounds and is known for its high resolving power, while GLC is favored for separating organic compounds due to its sensitivity and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in analytical chemistry for separating and analyzing complex mixtures.</p></p>
