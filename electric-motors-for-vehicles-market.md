<p><strong>The Electric Motors for Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor and Asynchronous Motor are two main types of electric motors used in vehicles. Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors have magnets embedded in the rotor, providing high efficiency and power density. Asynchronous Motors do not have magnets in the rotor, making them simpler and cheaper to manufacture. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors being more efficient but also more expensive, while Asynchronous Motors are less efficient but more cost-effective.</p></p>
