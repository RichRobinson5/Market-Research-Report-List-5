<p><p>Floating boat lifts are available in three main types: static-engineered, air assisted-engineered, and engineered for multi-hull vessels. Static-engineered lifts use a system of cables and pulleys to lift boats out of the water. Air assisted-engineered lifts use compressed air to lift boats, reducing the need for maintenance. Engineered for multi-hull vessels lifts are specifically designed to support multiple hulls, providing stability and security. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the size and type of boat being lifted.</p></p>
