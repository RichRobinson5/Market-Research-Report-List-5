<p><p>High dielectric plastic films are electrical insulating materials used in a wide range of applications. The four main types in the market are PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), and others. PTFE films are known for their high temperature resistance and chemical inertness. PET films are flexible and have good electrical properties. PP films are lightweight and have excellent moisture resistance. Other types include materials like polyimide and polycarbonate, each offering unique properties suitable for different applications.</p></p>
