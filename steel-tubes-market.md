<p><strong>The Steel Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded Tube</li><li>Seamless Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel tubes are available in two main market types: Welded Tube and Seamless Tube. Welded tubes are made by rolling a strip of steel into a tube shape and welding the edges together. They are cost-effective and widely used in construction and infrastructure projects. On the other hand, seamless tubes are made by piercing a solid billet of steel to create a seamless tube without any welded joints. Seamless tubes are known for their high strength and reliability, commonly used in demanding applications such as oil and gas industry.</p></p>
