MMA/Acrylate Copolymer processing aids are known for their excellent compatibility with PVC resins and ability to improve melt strength and reduce fusion time. MMA/Styrene Copolymer aids are valued for their high impact resistance and good dispersion properties. The "Others" category includes various types of processing aids that may offer unique benefits such as enhanced thermal stability or improved lubricating properties.</p></p>
