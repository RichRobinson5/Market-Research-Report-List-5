<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Side feeders are often classified into two main types based on the design of the screw mechanism: single screw and twin screw feeders. Single screw feeders have a single screw that rotates within the feeder to mix and deliver materials. Twin screw feeders, on the other hand, have two interlocking screws that work together to effectively handle and process a wider range of materials. Both types of feeders are commonly used in industries such as plastics, food processing, and pharmaceuticals to efficiently distribute materials throughout processing systems.</p></p>
