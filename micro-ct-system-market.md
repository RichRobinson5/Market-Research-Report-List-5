<p><p>A Micro CT System is commonly used for imaging small biological samples in both in vivo and ex vivo settings. In vivo imaging refers to capturing images inside a living organism, while ex vivo imaging involves imaging samples outside of a living organism. In vivo Micro CT Systems are used for studying anatomical structures and physiological processes in living organisms, while ex vivo Micro CT Systems are used for examining tissues, organs, and other biological samples removed from a living organism for further analysis.</p></p>
