<p><p>Hybrid cloud storage market is primarily segmented into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. In the Cloud-Based market, data is stored and managed off-site in a third-party cloud service provider's data centers. This enables scalability, cost-efficiency, and accessibility from any location. On the other hand, in the On-Premise market, data is stored and managed locally on dedicated servers within an organization's own data center. This provides greater control, security, and compliance with regulatory requirements. Both types of hybrid cloud storage solutions offer unique advantages and cater to different business needs.</p></p>
