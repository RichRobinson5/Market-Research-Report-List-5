<p><p>Steel plate market can be categorized into two main types: hot rolled steel plate and cold rolled steel plate. Hot rolled steel plate is processed at high temperatures, resulting in a rough surface finish and is ideal for structural applications. On the other hand, cold rolled steel plate is processed at lower temperatures, yielding a smoother surface finish and higher dimensional accuracy, making it suitable for applications requiring tight tolerances. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
