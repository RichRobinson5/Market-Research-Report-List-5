<p><p>The Vehicle Horn Market consists of three main types: Air Horns, Electronic Horns, and Electromagnetic Horns. Air Horns produce sound by forcing compressed air through a horn, creating a loud tone. Electronic Horns use electronic circuits to produce sound, offering a more modern and compact solution. Electromagnetic Horns produce sound through the vibration of a diaphragm using an electromagnet. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to different preferences and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
