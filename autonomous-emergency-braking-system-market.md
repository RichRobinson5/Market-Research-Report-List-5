Low Speed-City AEB Systems operate at lower speeds in urban areas to avoid accidents in heavy traffic. Pedestrian-VRU AEB Systems specifically target vulnerable road users such as pedestrians, cyclists, and motorcyclists to enhance their safety on the roads. Forward Emergency Braking helps prevent collisions by automatically applying the brakes when a potential front-end collision is detected. Multi-directional Braking is designed to provide protection from collisions in all directions by sensing obstacles from multiple angles and applying brakes accordingly.</p></p>
