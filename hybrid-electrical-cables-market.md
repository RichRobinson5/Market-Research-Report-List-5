<p><p>Hybrid electrical cables typically come in two types: Single-Mode and Multimode. Single-Mode cables are designed for long-distance communication with a single beam of light, making them ideal for telecommunications and networking applications. Multimode cables, on the other hand, are better suited for shorter distances and use multiple beams of light to carry data. Both types of cables are commonly used in data centers, telecommunications infrastructure, and industrial automation systems to transmit electrical signals efficiently and reliably.</p></p>
