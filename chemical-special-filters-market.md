<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Filters</li><li>Ion-exchange Resin Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical special filters include activated carbon filters and ion-exchange resin filters. Activated carbon filters are used to remove impurities and contaminants from water and air by adsorbing them onto the filter's surface. Ion-exchange resin filters work by exchanging ions in the water with ions in the resin, effectively removing harmful substances from the water. Both types of filters are essential in ensuring clean and safe water and air, making them crucial in various industries such as water treatment, air purification, and chemical processing.</p></p>
