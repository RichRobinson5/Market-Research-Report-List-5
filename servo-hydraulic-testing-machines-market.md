<p><p>Servo-hydraulic testing machines are used in various industries for different types of testing. Tension, compression, flexure, fatigue, fracture mechanics, damping properties, and vibration testing are the main market types for these machines. Tension testing measures the strength of materials under tension, while compression testing evaluates materials under compression. Flexure testing assesses the bending strength of materials. Fatigue testing determines the endurance limit of materials. Fracture mechanics testing studies the behavior of materials under stress. Damping properties testing measures the ability of materials to dissipate energy. Vibration testing analyzes the vibration resistance of materials.</p></p>
