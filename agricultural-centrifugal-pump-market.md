<p><p>The Agricultural Centrifugal Pump Market includes two main types: Single-stage Pump and Multi-stage Pump. Single-stage pumps are designed for applications where a high flow rate is required at a low discharge pressure. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, are used for applications where a high discharge pressure is needed. Single-stage pumps are simpler and more cost-effective, while multi-stage pumps are more efficient and suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types play a vital role in agricultural irrigation systems and water management in farming operations.</p></p>
