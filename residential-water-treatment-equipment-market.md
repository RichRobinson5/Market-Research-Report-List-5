<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market includes Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU systems are installed at specific faucets or fixtures to treat water only at that location, while POE systems treat all water entering a household. POU systems are typically more affordable and easy to install, making them popular for individual use. On the other hand, POE systems are larger and more complex, treating water for an entire house and are often used in areas with specific water quality issues.</p></p>
