<p><p>Ambulance vehicles market consists of four main types - Type I, Type II, Type III, and Type IV ambulances. Type I ambulances are built on truck chassis for durability and high weight capacity. Type II ambulances are smaller and more maneuverable, often used for non-emergency medical transport. Type III ambulances are built on a van chassis and are commonly used for emergency medical services. Type IV ambulances are specialty vehicles designed for specific medical uses, such as neonatal transport. Each type serves different purposes in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
