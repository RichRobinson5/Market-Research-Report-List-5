<p><p>Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is a type of polyethylene with a linear structure that provides high tensile strength and puncture resistance. There are three main types of LLDPE based on the comonomer used in the polymerization process: C4-LLDPE, C6-LLDPE, and C8-LLDPE. C4-LLDPE contains butene as a comonomer, while C6-LLDPE contains hexene, and C8-LLDPE contains octene. Each type offers different properties and is used in various applications such as packaging, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
