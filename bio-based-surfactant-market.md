Lipopeptides and Lipoproteins are surfactants with antimicrobial properties. Polymeric Surfactants are large molecules that reduce surface tension, and Particulate Biosurfactants are solid particles that reduce interfacial tension between substances. Each type offers unique benefits in various applications.</p></p>
