<p><p>Animal feed enzymes are categorized into different types based on their specific functions in the digestive system of animals. Phytases are enzymes that help in breaking down phytic acid in plant-based feeds, improving phosphorus utilization. Carbohydrases aid in the digestion of carbohydrates, while Proteases break down proteins into amino acids for better absorption. Other types of enzymes in the market include lipases, which help in digesting fats, and cellulases, which break down cellulose in plant-based feeds. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing nutrient utilization in animals.</p></p>
