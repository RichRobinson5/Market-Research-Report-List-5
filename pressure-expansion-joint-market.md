<p><p>Pressure expansion joints are used in piping systems to absorb thermal expansion and vibration, preventing damage to the system. Two-way pressure expansion joints allow movement in two directions, accommodating expansion and contraction along two axes. Three-way pressure expansion joints provide movement in three directions, allowing for greater flexibility in piping systems with complex layouts. Both types of expansion joints are essential in ensuring the integrity and longevity of piping systems by absorbing pressure and thermal stresses effectively.</p></p>
