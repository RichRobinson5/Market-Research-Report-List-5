<p><p>Feed mycotoxin binders are used in animal feed to prevent the harmful effects of mycotoxins. Different types of mycotoxin binders include HSCAS, Bentonite, Zeolites, and Polysaccharides. HSCAS binders are natural clay materials that have a high surface area for binding mycotoxins. Bentonite binders also have a high surface area and are effective in binding various mycotoxins. Zeolites are mineral-based binders known for their high selectivity in binding specific mycotoxins. Polysaccharide binders are derived from natural sources and are effective in binding a wide range of mycotoxins.</p></p>
