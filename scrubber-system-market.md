<p><p>Scrubber systems come in various types to help reduce emissions from ships. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to clean exhaust gases, while closed loop scrubbers circulate a liquid solution for cleaning. Hybrid scrubbers combine both open and closed loop technologies for more flexibility. Dry scrubbers use a dry sorbent to remove pollutants, while membrane scrubbers utilize a membrane to separate pollutants. Each type of scrubber system offers unique benefits and options for reducing emissions in marine operations.</p></p>
