<p><p>Synthetic calcium carbonate, commonly known as GCC (Ground Calcium Carbonate) and PCC (Precipitated Calcium Carbonate), are two main types found in the market. GCC is produced by grinding and processing natural limestone, while PCC is synthesized through a chemical precipitation process. GCC is used in various industries such as paper, plastics, paints, and adhesives. PCC is preferred for its smaller particle size and higher brightness, making it suitable for industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and personal care products.</p></p>
