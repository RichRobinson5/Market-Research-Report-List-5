<p><p>Pharmaceutical pump market offers various types of pumps including centrifugal pump, positive displacement pump, jet pump, and air lift pump. Centrifugal pumps use centrifugal force to generate pressure, ideal for transferring liquids with low viscosity. Positive displacement pumps are suitable for transferring viscous fluids by trapping and displacing a fixed volume of liquid. Jet pumps utilize fluid momentum for pumping purposes, while air lift pumps use compressed air to lift liquids. Each type of pump has unique characteristics to cater to different pharmaceutical pumping requirements.</p></p>
