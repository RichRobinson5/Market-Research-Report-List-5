<p><p>Inorganic electrochromic materials play a crucial role in the development of smart windows and displays. The market is primarily categorized into four types: WO3, MoO3, TiO3, and Others. WO3 (tungsten trioxide) is widely used for its excellent electrochromic properties. MoO3 (molybdenum trioxide) is known for its high stability and durability. TiO3 (titanium trioxide) offers good optical and electrochemical performance. The "Others" category includes various inorganic materials like NiO, V2O5, and CeO2 that are also used in electrochromic applications.</p></p>
