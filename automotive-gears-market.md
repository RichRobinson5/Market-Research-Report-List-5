<p><p>The automotive gears market can be broadly categorized into various types including spur gear, helical gear, bevel gear, worm gear, gear rack, and others. Spur gears are the simplest type and are used in applications requiring high power transmission. Helical gears offer smoother and quieter operation compared to spur gears. Bevel gears are used in applications where the shafts intersect at an angle. Worm gears are used for high gear ratios. Gear racks are used for linear motion applications. Other types of gears offer specialized solutions for specific automotive applications.</p></p>
