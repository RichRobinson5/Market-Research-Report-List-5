<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass-to-metal seals are used in various applications where a hermetic seal between glass and metal is required. Matched seals are a type where the coefficient of thermal expansion of the glass and metal are similar, allowing for a strong bond. Compression seals, on the other hand, use a compressive force to create a tight seal between the materials. Both types are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their ability to provide a secure and reliable seal.</p></p>
