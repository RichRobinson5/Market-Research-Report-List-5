<p><p>Vegetative roof systems come in three main types: Extensive, Semi-intensive, and Intensive. </p><p>- Extensive types are low-maintenance and typically consist of shallow soil depth, requiring minimal upkeep and irrigation. They are ideal for lightweight structures and have a lower cost.</p><p>- Semi-intensive types have a moderate level of maintenance, with deeper soil depths allowing for a wider variety of plant species and better stormwater management.</p><p>- Intensive types are the most complex and high-maintenance, with deep soil depths and a diverse range of plants, trees, and even recreational spaces. They provide the most environmental benefits but require significant upkeep and investment.</p></p>
