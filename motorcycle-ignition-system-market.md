<p><p>The motorcycle ignition system market is categorized into three main types: mechanical ignition system, electronic ignition system, and others. Mechanical ignition systems use points and condensers to control the timing of the spark, while electronic ignition systems use electronic control units (ECUs) to precisely time the ignition spark. Other types of ignition systems may include capacitive discharge ignition (CDI) systems or inductive ignition systems. Each type of ignition system offers unique advantages in terms of performance, reliability, and efficiency.</p></p>
