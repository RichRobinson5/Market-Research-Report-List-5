<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems can be categorized into three main types: Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), and Rack-EPS (R-EPS). C-EPS utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering column, providing assistance to the driver. P-EPS incorporates the motor directly onto the steering gear, while R-EPS integrates the motor into the steering rack. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, performance, and cost, catering to different vehicle requirements and driving preferences.</p></p>
