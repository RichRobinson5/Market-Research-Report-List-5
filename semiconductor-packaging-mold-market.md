<p><strong>The Semiconductor Packaging Mold Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transfer Molds</li><li>Compression Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor packaging molds are essential in the production of semiconductor devices. Transfer molds are used to transfer the mold compound onto the semiconductor device, ensuring precise placement and encapsulation. Compression molds, on the other hand, apply pressure to the mold compound to achieve the desired shape and size. Both types of molds play a crucial role in the semiconductor packaging process by ensuring the proper encapsulation and protection of the semiconductor devices, ultimately contributing to their performance and longevity.</p></p>

SiP integrates multiple components into a single package, streamlining the manufacturing process and improving overall system performance.</p></p>
