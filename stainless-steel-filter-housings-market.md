<p><p>Stainless steel filter housings are commonly categorized into two main types: side-entry type and top-entry type. Side-entry type filter housings are designed for easy access to the filter elements from the side, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, top-entry type filter housings allow for easy access to the filter elements from the top, typically offering larger capacities and higher flow rates. Both types of filter housings provide efficient filtration solutions for various industrial processes.</p></p>
