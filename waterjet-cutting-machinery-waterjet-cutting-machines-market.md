<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterjet cutting machinery includes two main types: pressurized water and abrasive mixture. Pressurized water cutting machines use a high-pressure stream of water to cut through various materials like rubber, foam, and soft metals. Abrasive mixture cutting machines add abrasive particles, such as garnet, to the water stream to increase cutting power, making it capable of slicing through harder materials like steel, glass, and stone. Both types of waterjet cutting machines offer a precise, clean, and versatile method of material cutting in various industries.</p></p>
