<p><strong>The Automotive Electrically Driven Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Pump</li><li>Separate Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive electrically driven oil pump market includes two main types: Integrated Pump and Separate Pump. Integrated Pump systems are built directly into the engine block and are driven by an electric motor that is integrated within the pump assembly. Separate Pump systems have a standalone pump that is driven by an external electric motor. Integrated pumps are often more compact and efficient, while Separate pumps offer more flexibility in design and placement. Both types provide efficient oil circulation and aid in improving engine performance.</p></p>
