<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Optical Metrology Market Types refer to different methods used for performing immunological tests. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) detects light emission from a chemical reaction. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) uses enzymes to produce a fluorescent signal for detection. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) involves using enzymes to detect the presence of antibodies or antigens. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) uses radioactive isotopes to measure the levels of antigens or antibodies in a sample. These methods are commonly used in clinical diagnostics and research laboratories.</p></p>
