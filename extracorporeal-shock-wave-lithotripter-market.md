<p><p>Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripter systems are utilized for non-invasive treatment of kidney stones. There are three main types of lithotripters: electrohydraulic, electromagnetic, and piezoelectric. Electrohydraulic lithotripters rely on electrical discharges to generate shock waves, while electromagnetic lithotripters use electromagnetic coils. Piezoelectric lithotripters create shock waves through piezoelectric crystals. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of effectiveness, precision, and cost, offering healthcare providers a variety of options for treating kidney stones.</p></p>
