<p><p>Railway Overhead Catenary System (OCS) includes two main types in the market - Flexible and Rigid. Flexible systems use tensioned wires supported by a series of structures, allowing for easier installation over uneven terrain. Rigid systems use a solid bar or tube as the overhead conductor, providing greater stability but requiring more support structures. Both systems have their own advantages and are chosen based on factors such as terrain, cost, and maintenance requirements in the railway electrification market.</p></p>
