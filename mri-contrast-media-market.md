<p><p>MRI contrast media market can be segmented into injection type and oral type. Injection type contrast media are administered intravenously to enhance the visibility of specific tissues or organs during MRI scans. They are fast-acting and provide immediate results. On the other hand, oral type contrast media are ingested orally prior to the MRI scan to highlight the gastrointestinal tract. They are slower-acting but are useful for studying the digestive system and evaluating abnormalities in the stomach and intestines.</p></p>
