They utilize the principle of heat transfer to determine the flow rate based on the amount of heat required to maintain a constant temperature difference between two points in the flow stream. On the other hand, Calorimetric Flow Meters for gases are specifically designed to measure the flow rate of gases in industrial applications. They operate on the same principle as liquid flow meters, but are optimized for gas flow measurements.</p></p>
