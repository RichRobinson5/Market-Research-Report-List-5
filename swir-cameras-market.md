<p><p>SWIR cameras are classified into two main types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras capture 2D images with high spatial resolution, making them ideal for applications such as surveillance and reconnaissance. On the other hand, SWIR linear cameras are designed for capturing one-dimensional images with high sensitivity, often used in industrial applications such as sorting and inspection. Both types of SWIR cameras offer unique advantages and are essential tools in various industries for capturing images in the shortwave infrared spectrum.</p></p>
