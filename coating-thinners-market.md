<p><p>Coating thinners market types include mineral spirits, turpentine, naphtha, toluene, methyl ethyl ketone (MEK), dimethylformamide (DMF), 2-butoxyethanol, and others. Mineral spirits are commonly used for oil-based paint thinning, while turpentine is used for cleaning brushes and thinning oil-based paints. Naphtha is a fast-drying thinner used in industrial coatings. Toluene is a strong solvent used in lacquers and paints. MEK is used in epoxy and lacquer thinners. DMF is used in specialty coatings. 2-Butoxyethanol is used in water-based coatings and as a cleaning solvent.</p></p>
