<p><p>Aircraft airspeed indicators come in two main types: analog and digital. Analog airspeed indicators use a needle and dial display to show the speed of the aircraft. These are traditional and widely used in aviation. On the other hand, digital airspeed indicators have a digital screen that displays the speed numerically. They offer a more precise and easy-to-read display for pilots. Both types are essential instruments for pilots to monitor the speed of their aircraft during flight.</p></p>
