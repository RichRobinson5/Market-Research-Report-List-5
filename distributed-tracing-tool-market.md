<p><strong>The Distributed Tracing Tool Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Distributed Tracing</li><li>Infinite Tracing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard Distributed Tracing tools are designed to track and monitor the flow of requests across various services and components in a distributed system. These tools provide visibility into the performance and behavior of applications, helping to diagnose and troubleshoot issues efficiently. On the other hand, Infinite Tracing tools offer advanced capabilities for capturing and analyzing every single request in a system, providing even deeper insights into the performance and behavior of applications. This can be particularly useful for complex and high-volume environments where standard tracing tools may not provide sufficient visibility.</p></p>
