<p><strong>The Automotive Heads-up Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected</li><li>Combiner Projected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Heads-up Display (HUD) systems come in two main types: windshield projected and combiner projected. In windshield projected displays, the information is directly projected onto the windshield in front of the driver, allowing them to view crucial data like speed and navigation instructions without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner projected displays, on the other hand, utilize a separate transparent panel or combiner to reflect the information in the driver's line of sight. Both types aim to enhance safety and convenience by providing important information while driving.</p></p>
