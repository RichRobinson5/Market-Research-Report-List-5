<p><p>Lawn mower batteries are available in two main types - lead-acid batteries and Li-Ion batteries. Lead-acid batteries are more traditional, cost-effective, and reliable, but they are heavier and have a shorter lifespan compared to Li-Ion batteries. Li-Ion batteries, on the other hand, are lighter, more energy-efficient, and have a longer lifespan. They are also more environmentally friendly as they do not contain toxic materials like lead. Both types of batteries are commonly used in lawn mowers, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
