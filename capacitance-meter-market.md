<p><ul><li>Non-bridge Meters</li><li>Bridges Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitance meter market can be divided into two main types: non-bridge meters and bridge meters. Non-bridge meters are simpler and more cost-effective, relying on alternative methods to measure capacitance such as direct measurement or frequency conversion. On the other hand, bridge meters provide more accurate results by balancing the unknown capacitance against a known standard value using a bridge circuit. Despite being more complex and expensive, bridge meters are commonly used in industries where precision is crucial.</p></p>
