<p><p>Spray polyurethane foam (SPF) for building insulation comes in two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell SPF is a lighter and less dense option, providing excellent sound insulation and flexibility. Closed cell SPF is denser and offers higher R-values, making it ideal for areas requiring a strong moisture barrier. Both types of SPF provide excellent thermal insulation and air sealing properties, helping to improve energy efficiency and reduce heating and cooling costs in buildings.</p></p>
