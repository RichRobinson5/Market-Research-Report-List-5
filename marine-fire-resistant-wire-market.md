<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multicore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine fire resistant wire market consists of two main types - single core and multicore wires. Single core wires have a single conducting core surrounded by insulation, primarily used in applications where flexibility is not a concern. On the other hand, multicore wires consist of multiple cores within a single cable sheath, offering flexibility and easier installation in complex systems. Both types are designed to withstand high temperatures and provide reliable electrical connections in marine environments while ensuring safety in case of fire emergencies.</p></p>
