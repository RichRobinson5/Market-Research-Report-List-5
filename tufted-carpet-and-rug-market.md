<p><p>The Tufted Carpet and Rug market can be classified into different types based on construction techniques. Woven carpets and rugs are made on a loom using various fibers. Needle felt carpets are made by interlocking fibers with barbed needles. Knotted carpets are created by tying yarn on a base material. Tufted carpets are formed by punching yarn through a primary backing. Other types include machine-made and hand-tufted carpets. Each type offers unique qualities and characteristics in terms of appearance, durability, and cost.</p></p>
