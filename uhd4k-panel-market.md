<p><p>UHD/4K panels are available in two main types based on the technology used: LED and LCD. LED technology utilizes Light Emitting Diodes to provide backlighting for the display, resulting in brighter and more energy-efficient screens. On the other hand, LCD technology uses Liquid Crystal Displays to manipulate light passing through the screen, offering high resolution and color accuracy. Both types of panels offer high-definition visuals, but LED panels are often preferred for their superior brightness and efficiency.</p></p>
