<p><p>Arrayed Waveguide Grating (AWG) devices are used in wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) systems to separate and combine different wavelengths of light. There are two main types of AWG devices: Thermal AWG and Athermal AWG. </p><p>Thermal AWG devices require temperature control to maintain precise wavelength spacing, making them more complex and expensive. Athermal AWG devices, on the other hand, are designed to operate without temperature control, making them more cost-effective and easier to deploy in various environments. Both types play a crucial role in WDM systems for telecommunications and data networking applications.</p></p>
