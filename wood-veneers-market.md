<p><p>Wood veneers are thin slices of real wood that are used to cover surfaces for decorative purposes. The market for wood veneers is divided into different types including raw veneer, paper backed veneer, phenolic backed veneer, laid up veneer, and reconstituted veneer. Raw veneer is the natural form of wood veneers, while paper backed veneer has a thin layer of paper attached for added stability. Phenolic backed veneer is reinforced with a phenolic resin backing, laid up veneer is multiple veneers glued together, and reconstituted veneer is made from recycled wood fibers.</p></p>
