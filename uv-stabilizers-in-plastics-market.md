<p><p>UV Stabilizers are additives used in plastics to prevent degradation caused by exposure to ultraviolet radiation. There are different types of UV stabilizers in the market, including Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV Absorbers, Quenchers, and Others. HALS work by scavenging free radicals generated by UV exposure, UV Absorbers absorb UV radiation and convert it into heat, Quenchers deactivate excited states, and the Others category includes different mechanisms such as nanoparticles and inorganic pigments that provide UV protection to plastics.</p></p>
